About

Enhance your vocabulary without breaking your focus.

for the student, dic{t}ext is a simple chrome extension that will take a highlighted word and automatically retrieve its definition, respective synonyms & antonyms, provides an example of the word used in a sentence, as well as listing hot articles where this word is frequently used.

This simple interface overlays the chrome page and saves the user time to look up an uncertain word’s definition and allows to work without breaking their workflow or focus.
