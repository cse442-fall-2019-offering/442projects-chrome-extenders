About

This story begins with our not so lucky hero John. Whenever he would do school work, he would often have to look up the meaning of certain words while studying, or search for longer synonyms to add length to his essays. Being not the most disciplined student, he often got distracted and pulled away from his work during this task. Unable to remain diligent in his work, his quality of work suffered and his grades ultimately dropped and he ultimately flunked out of college.

Whether you’re a student or a professional, having to break your workflow to look up a word’s definition can break your focus and in turn cause unnecessary distractions. In an effort to help streamline workflow and improve performance, we created dic{t}ext! A simple and easy to use extension to add to your chrome browser. With this simple and easy to use extension, you can easily look up a word’s definition with ease! As simple as pressing Ctrl + Shift + 9! And in case you need synonyms or antonyms, those are available too along with an example of the word used in a sentence.

You can also translate words into different languages! Currently dic{t}ext supports translation into 9 different languages. With a simple selection one can easily find the translation of a word into their choice of French, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Danish, German, Arabic, Chinese or Japanese. Never again do you have to break focus from your work and compromise your attention. All thanks to dic{t}ext!
